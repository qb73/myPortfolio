About Me

I am a resident Potsdam Mew York. I have many passions, but the number 1 thing that stands out is my hobby of game-making. These are generally physical ones that use objects and descriptions of games I have made. Games that I've created are Shellhole (TBS), Preservation (Card Game), World at War (Simulated), to name a few. I very much am proud of my heritage, where my father came from England to live here, and I pride myself from that association.
